Ark Survival Evolved is a video game in which you can tame wild dinosaurs, to later defeat bosses, destroy bases, and fight against other players who are online, you can also craft your items or weapons to be well equipped, and be able to defend yourself at any time.

    PROS

  • It's a fun video game.
  • You can make friends while playing.
  • It's Very entertaining.
  • Is not expensive.
  • IS Very different to other video games, or "Original".

    CONS

  • There are too many hackers, with aimbot.
  • You need a lot of time to play.
  • You can lose all your progress if someone raids your base.
  • The game needs a lot of storage.
  • You need a good PC, or console to play well.
